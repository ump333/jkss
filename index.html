About Our School

Our History

Founded in 1987, Janakalyan Secondary School has been a beacon of quality education in our community. We started with just 50 students and have grown to serve over 1,200 students annually, maintaining our commitment to academic excellence and character development.

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing environment that fosters intellectual curiosity, creativity, and ethical values, preparing students to become responsible global citizens and leaders of tomorrow.

All News & Events

June 15, 2023

Annual Sports Day

Our annual sports day was a grand success with participation from all grades. Students competed in various events including track and field, basketball, and traditional games. The event was inaugurated by our principal, Mr. Sharma, who emphasized the importance of physical fitness along with academic excellence.

The closing ceremony featured prize distribution and cultural performances. Congratulations to all participants and winners!

July 5, 2023

Science Fair Winners

Our students made us proud at the regional science fair with their innovative projects. The team of Riya Sharma and Amit Kumar won first prize for their project "Solar-Powered Water Purification System" in the Environmental Science category.

Other notable projects included "AI-Based Waste Segregation" by Neha Patel and "Low-Cost Braille Printer" by Arjun Thapa. We congratulate all participants for their hard work and creativity.

August 20, 2023

New Computer Lab

Our new state-of-the-art computer lab is now operational with 50 high-performance computers equipped with the latest software. The lab was inaugurated by the District Education Officer, Mr. Ramesh Poudel.

The lab features dedicated workstations for programming, graphic design, and robotics. We've also introduced new courses in Python programming and AI basics for interested students.

September 10, 2023

Teacher's Day Celebration

Students organized a special program to honor our teachers on Teacher's Day. The event included cultural performances, speeches, and awards for outstanding teachers. Our principal praised the student organizers for their efforts in making the event memorable.
